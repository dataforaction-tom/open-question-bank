# Question Bank

Question Bank turns a messy pile of questions from a community into a short, trustworthy,
prioritised list — the questions that matter most, agreed on by the people who asked them.

Anyone can submit a question. From there it's checked over, sometimes improved for clarity,
checked for quality, and put up against other questions in head-to-head comparisons until a
ranked list emerges. Every step is visible: you can always see why a question ended up where it
did.

It's built for communities, councils, and organisations who want to know "what should we actually
be asking?" before they spend time and money answering anything.

## Getting started

- **Want to add a question?** Go to **Submit** and write what's on your mind. See the
  [Submitting a question](user-guide.md#submitting-a-question) section for what happens next.
- **Want to see what's already there?** Visit **Browse** to search and explore the question bank,
  or **Campaigns** to see active and finished prioritisation rounds.
- **Want to help rank questions?** Open a campaign that's "open for comparison" and start judging
  pairs — see [Comparing and ranking questions](user-guide.md#comparing-and-ranking-questions).
- **Running this for an organisation?** See the [Admin guide](user-guide.md#admin-guide) section
  for moderation, refinement, quality checks, and setting up campaigns.
